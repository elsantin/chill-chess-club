# Product Overview

**Chill Chess Club** is a professional landing page for online chess instruction services.

## Core Purpose

Convert website visitors into students by presenting a clear narrative: instructor introduction → course offerings → teaching methodology → FAQ → contact.

## Target Audience

- Chess beginners seeking personalized online instruction
- Intermediate players looking to improve their game
- Spanish and English speaking students worldwide

## Key Features

- Bilingual support (Spanish/English) with automatic language detection
- Two course tiers: Beginner ($13/hour) and Intermediate ($16/hour)
- Single-page application with smooth scroll navigation
- Contact form with validation for student inquiries
- Blog and resources sections for content marketing
- Dark theme with vibrant sunset-inspired color palette

## Instructor

Santiago Narváez - Chess instructor based in Isla de Margarita, Venezuela, offering 1-on-1 online classes via Chess.com and Lichess.

## Business Model

Direct student acquisition through the website, with pricing displayed upfront to qualify leads before contact.
